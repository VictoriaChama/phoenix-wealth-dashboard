Product Requirements Document (PRD) + Technical Stack

Product Name (working): Phoenix Wealth
Version: MVP v1
Primary User: You (with future multi-user support)
Platform: Web (cross-platform, mobile-friendly)

1. PRODUCT VISION
Problem Statement
Personal wealth tracking is often fragmented across spreadsheets, bank apps, investment platforms, and notes. This makes it difficult to:
See net worth clearly
Track progress toward financial goals
Understand cash flow patterns over time

Solution
A centralized wealth dashboard that consolidates:
Net worth
Assets & liabilities
Cash flow
Goals
Trends & insights

Built initially for manual data entry (Excel-style), with future automation potential.

2. PRODUCT GOALS (SMART)
Deliver a fully functional MVP by September 2026
Replicate and improve the functionality of your existing Excel dashboard

Serve as:
A portfolio project
A personal finance management tool
A foundation for future fintech products
